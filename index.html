This project aims to help people manage their time better using a user-friendly tool. By analysing their leisure activities and displaying this information through an easily digestible pie chart, I want to make it easy for users to see how they spend their time. The goal is to provide insights and encourage a balanced lifestyle. Users will be able to make well-informed decisions based on what part of their daily life they should minimise to replace with a more fulfilling habit like exercising or reading a book. This project hopes to impact people positively leading to increased productivity, reduced stress, and enhanced overall well-being.
